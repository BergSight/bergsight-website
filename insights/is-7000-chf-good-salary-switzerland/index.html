Is CHF 7,000 a good salary in Switzerland?

It depends who you ask — and where you live. Here is an honest look at what CHF 7,000 per month actually means in Switzerland in 2026.

By Lukas 5 min read Written by a human
The short answer
Almost — CHF 7,000 gross per month is just fractionally below the Swiss national median of CHF 7,024. In practical terms it is right at the average. For a single person it is a comfortable salary in most regions. For a family, or in Zurich and Geneva specifically, it requires more careful budgeting.

Above the median
The Swiss national median gross salary is CHF 7,024 per month (FSO 2024). CHF 7,000 sits just below this — meaning you earn more than roughly half the working population.
Region matters
The median in Zurich is CHF 7,502 and in Ticino CHF 5,708. The same salary means something very different depending on where you work and live.
