Retirement in Switzerland — Utopia?

Switzerland ranks among the best places in the world to live. But even here, retirement provision keeps people up at night. Here's why — and what you can do about it.

Pillar 1
State pension
AHV / OASI
Mandatory state pension funded by contributions from employees, employers, and the government. Provides a basic income in retirement.
Pillar 2
Occupational pension
BVG / LPP
Employer-based pension fund. Together with Pillar 1, aims to provide around 60% of your final gross salary in retirement.
Pillar 3
Private provision
3a (tied) / 3b (flexible)
Voluntary private savings, partially tax-deductible (3a). The most flexible lever for closing the gap between Pillars 1+2 and your retirement goals.
