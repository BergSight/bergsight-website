Compound Growth
Projector

See how your savings grow over time with the power of compound interest. Adjust your initial investment, monthly contributions, annual return rate, and time horizon to model your financial future.

Investing Savings Long-term planning CHF
How it works

Three inputs, one powerful insight

01
Set your parameters
Enter your starting amount, how much you plan to save each month, your expected annual return, and how many years you want to model.
02
See your growth curve
The chart shows three lines: your total portfolio value, your contributions alone, and the real inflation-adjusted value — so you can see what your money is actually worth.
03
Experiment freely
Try different scenarios — what if you save CHF 200 more per month? What if returns are 5% instead of 7%? Every change updates instantly.
