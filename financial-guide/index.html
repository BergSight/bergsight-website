Swiss personal finance guide

Am I doing the right things
with my money?

A clear, step-by-step guide to Swiss personal finance — from building a budget to long-term investing. Expand each step to explore what to do and why it matters in the Swiss context.

This guide is for general information only, not financial advice. Every person’s situation is different — your income, family, tax position, and goals all influence what is right for you. Use this as a starting point, and seek independent professional advice for significant decisions.
A final thought
Money is a tool, not the destination.

Financial security matters — but so does everything else. Your health, your relationships, your family, your friends, and the experiences that make life meaningful are not line items in a spreadsheet. A good financial plan creates freedom, not constraint. Build in room for spontaneity, generosity, and enjoyment. Work hard, save thoughtfully, invest wisely — and then live your life.
