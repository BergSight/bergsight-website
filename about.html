Our story

Built for Switzerland.
Independent, simple, and free.

BergSight exists because good financial planning tools — truly built for Switzerland — were nowhere to be found for free.

BergSight was built to fill a gap: genuinely independent financial planning tools, designed specifically for Switzerland, that anyone can use without signing up, paying, or sharing their data. No account needed, no agenda behind the numbers — just clarity.

Most financial planning tools available online were built for other markets. BergSight is different — built from the ground up for the Swiss financial context, CHF-denominated, and designed with both long-term residents and newly arrived expats in mind. Not adapted from another market. Built for this one.

Designed and developed in Zürich by Lukas, an insights and analytics professional with over 15 years' experience in market research and strategy. That background — rigorous with numbers, focused on making complexity understandable — shapes every tool on this site. BergSight is an independent project with no institutional backing and no financial products to sell. Its only purpose is to inform.
