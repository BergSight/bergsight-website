Privacy Policy

BergSight is built on a simple principle: your financial data is yours. We don't collect it, store it, or share it.

Last updated: April 2026 · BergSight, Zürich, Switzerland

The short version: BergSight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data you enter into our tools. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. When you close the page, nothing is retained.

Who we are

BergSight is an independent financial information platform based in Zürich, Switzerland. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of bergsight.com.

Data we do not collect

When you use BergSight planning tools — such as the Compound Growth Projector — all inputs and calculations are processed locally in your browser. No financial figures, personal details, or calculation results are transmitted to BergSight or any third party. We have no visibility into what numbers you enter or what results you receive.

BergSight does not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information to use any tool on this site.

Data we may collect

BergSight uses standard web analytics to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate. This may include anonymised data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, and approximate geographic location at country level. This data is used solely to improve the site and cannot be used to identify individual users.

BergSight uses Cloudflare to deliver and protect the website. Cloudflare may process certain connection data as part of its network infrastructure. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Cookies

BergSight uses a single functional cookie to remember your language preference. This cookie stores only your selected language (e.g. "en", "de", "fr", or "it") and contains no personal data. It is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with any third party.

No advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies are set by BergSight.

Third-party services

BergSight uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when loading the site, which may transmit your IP address to Google. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

The interactive tools on BergSight are hosted on a dedicated server. No personal or financial data entered into the tools is stored on this server.

Your rights

As BergSight does not collect or store personal data, there is typically nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have a specific concern or request, please contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@bergsight.com.
