Important notes on these estimates
Cantonal and municipal tax figures are approximations based on 2024 published rates for a single person in the cantonal capital, using standard deductions. Actual tax bills depend on your municipality, full deduction profile, and individual circumstances. The pension fund (BVG/Pillar 2) contribution is estimated based on your age bracket — actual rates vary by employer and fund. This calculator does not account for health insurance premiums, which are paid separately in Switzerland. Results are for guidance only and do not constitute financial or tax advice.
