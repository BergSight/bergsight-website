16.8%
What counts as saving? Monthly savings includes anything you deliberately set aside: bank transfers to savings, investment contributions, Pillar 3a deposits, and voluntary pension top-ups. Regular debt repayments beyond minimums also count. The Swiss average of 16.8% of gross income is from the FSO Household Budget Survey 2023.
—
These results are benchmarks for general orientation only. Wealth and savings are highly personal and depend on many factors this tool cannot capture: inheritance, property values, self-employment income, family circumstances, and personal goals. Being above or below the median is not inherently good or bad. Use this as a starting point for reflection, not a judgment. Always consult a qualified financial adviser for personalised guidance.
